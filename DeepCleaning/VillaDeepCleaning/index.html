What Distinguishes Emirates Cleaning Services?

  • Expert Deep Cleaning: We surpass surface cleaning, thoroughly sanitizing every area of your villa from top to bottom. Our skilled team employs specialized equipment and methods to eliminate even the most persistent dirt and buildup, guaranteeing a thoroughly clean and hygienic atmosphere.
  • Customized Cleaning Plans: Each villa is distinct, and we customize our deep cleaning services to match your specific requirements and preferences. We provide tailored strategies to accommodate your demands, whether you want a quick refresh or a comprehensive makeover.
  • Unwavering Quality: To provide a secure and dependable cleaning that lasts, we only use the best cleaning supplies and tools. We support environmental responsibility and make use of environmentally friendly products whenever we can.
  • Impeccable Attention to Detail: Our team is notable for its meticulous attention to detail, guaranteeing that no spot is missed and that your villa is spotless and shining from the moment we depart.
  • Unmatched Service: We are dedicated to providing outstanding customer service. From our first meeting to the last walk-through, we're here to address your questions and handle any issues.

Our Villa Deep Cleaning Services Include:

  • Bedrooms, Living Areas, and Common Spaces: Thorough cleaning of walls, corners, and baseboards (accessible areas), careful cleaning of cabinet shelves and drawers (accessible areas), detailed cleaning of light fixtures, ceiling lights, and spotlights (up to 3.5m high), dusting and cleaning of AC thermostats and ventilation grills (up to 3.5m high), and thorough cleaning of wooden doors and frames.
  • Toilets and Shower Areas: In-depth cleaning of cabinet shelves and drawers (accessible parts), careful cleaning of vanity countertops, thorough cleaning and descaling of sinks, drainage strainers, showerheads, and hand rinsers, shining of mirrors and glass doors, and meticulous cleaning of bathtubs, toilet seats, shower areas, and walls.
  • Balconies: Deep cleaning of floors with a single-disc scrubbing machine and wet/dry vacuum (not suitable for marble and wood floors) and careful cleaning of handrails and drainage strainers.
  • Kitchens and Dining Halls: Deep cleaning of cabinet shelves and drawers (accessible parts), careful cleaning and descaling of sinks, faucets, and drainage strainers, thorough cleaning of kitchen countertops, walls, corners, baseboards, and floors (accessible areas), cleaning of light fixtures, ceiling lights, and spotlights (up to 3.5m high).
  • Flooring and Staircases: Deep cleaning of vinyl, concrete, and ceramic floors with scrubbing machines and wet/dry vacuums, careful cleaning of marble floors and stairs with vacuums and dry mops, and thorough cleaning of handrails, corners, and edges.
  • Internal/External: Internal glass door and window deep cleaning (below 3.5 meters) and external glass door and window deep cleaning (below 7 meters).
