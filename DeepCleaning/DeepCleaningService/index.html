Deep Cleaning - Scope of Work

  • Bedroom/Living Room/Common Areas:
    • Deep Cleaning of walls, corners, and skirting (accessible parts).
    • Deep Cleaning of cabinet shelves and drawers (accessible parts).
    • Dust and wipe AC thermostat and AC ventilation grills (up to 3.5m high).
    • Dust and wipe wooden doors and frames.
    • Deep Cleaning of headboard frame (solid part), legs, and under the beds (accessible areas only).
  • Toilet and Shower Area:
    • Deep Cleaning of cabinet, shelves, and drawers (accessible parts).
    • Deep Cleaning of vanity countertop area.
    • Deep Cleaning of sink, drainage strainer, showerhead, and hand rinser.
    • Deep Cleaning and shine mirrors and glass doors.
    • Deep Cleaning of the bathtub, toilet seat, shower area, and walls.
    • Deep Cleaning of fixtures, ceiling light, spotlight, and exhaust fan diffuser.
  • Balcony:
    • Deep Cleaning of the floor using a single-disc scrubbing machine, wet/dry machine (not applicable for marble and wooden floors).
    • Deep Cleaning of handrails and drainage strainer.
  • Kitchen/Dining Hall:
    • Deep Cleaning of cabinet shelves and drawers.
    • Deep Cleaning of sink, faucets, and drainage strainer.
    • Deep Cleaning of kitchen countertop.
    • Deep Cleaning of walls, corners, skirting, and floor.
    • Deep Cleaning of light fixtures, ceiling light, and spotlight.
    • Dust and wipe the AC thermostat and AC ventilation grills.
    • Deep Cleaning of and degrease the kitchen hood.
    • Remove external grease marks and oil marks.
  • Flooring/Staircase:
    • Vacuum and mop the floor.
    • Deep Cleaning of marble-type floors using a vacuum and dry mopping.
    • Deep Cleaning of handrails, corners, and edges.
